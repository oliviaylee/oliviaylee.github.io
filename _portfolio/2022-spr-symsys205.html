--- title: "Predictive Processing: Efficiently processing high-dimensional, multimodal inputs" excerpt: "Final paper for Stanford's SYMSYS 205: The Philosophy and Science of Perception (Spring 2022)" collection: portfolio --- I argue for the plausibility of the predictive processing framework over the standard bottom-up model of perception, especially in the context of efficiently processing high-dimensional multimodal inputs, where the qualitative space of each modality has unique dimensionality and structure.
