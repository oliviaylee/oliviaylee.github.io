--- title: "Model Predictive Curiosity" excerpt: "Final poster and report for Stanford's PSYCH 240A: Curiosity in Artificial Intelligence (Spring 2022)" collection: portfolio --- Our team proposed Model Predictive Curiosity (MPCu), a framework that backpropagates on curiosity values predicted by a forward dynamics model to select curiosity-maximizing actions. We demonstrated the capability of MPCu to optimize for high-curiosity action values and enrich multi-object interactions in Box2D environment.
